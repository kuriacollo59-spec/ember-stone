Our Story

Where fire
meets flavour

Nestled in the heart of Nairobi, Ember & Oak was born from a deep reverence for open-flame cooking and the rich, earthy pantry of East Africa. Every dish is a conversation between fire, time, and the finest seasonal ingredients.

Our chef sources directly from local farms each morning, crafting a menu that evolves with the land. The result is cuisine that is simultaneously rooted and surprising — familiar enough to comfort, refined enough to astonish.
